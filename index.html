ABOUT ME

Hi, I am David Tang, a third-year Electrical Engineering student primarily interested in analog and digital circuit design. Throughout my academic journey, I have undertaken leadership roles in diverse teams, varying in size from 4 to 20 members, encompassing both students and professionals. Additionally, my comprehensive experience extends beyond academic realms, encompassing project management, customer service, and technical presentation.


Expected to graduate from Arizona State University with a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering in December 2024, I concurrently anticipate the completion of my Master's degree, with a focus on microelectronic circuit design, in the subsequent year.


In my free time, I like to translate video subtitles, make digital illustrations, and keep myself up-to-date on sinosphere archaeology findings.
